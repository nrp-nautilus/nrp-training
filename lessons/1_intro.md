## How to ask for help

Once you begin to use NRP on your own, you may have more questions about the platform. Here is the recommended flow for requesting help. 

1. Reach out to your namespace admin. You can find this by going to [https://nrp.ai/namespaces](https://nrp.ai/namespaces), selecting your namespaces and scrolling to the users section. 
![ns1](images/ns1.png)
![ns2](images/ns2.png)

2. The Nautilus support channel. Instructions to join can be found at [https://nrp.ai/contact/](https://nrp.ai/contact/). You can jopin either via Slack or Element/Matrix.
::: important
  It is intended that **all users** join the support chat. Important information is shared there and it is the best way to get help from the admin team.
:::
